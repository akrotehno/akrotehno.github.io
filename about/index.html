About

We love to focus on clear targets related to joy and love that all of us enjoy after successful practices in the art world.

In Greek (Ακρότεχνο) means at the edge of art.

History

Akrotehno started on how to develop and improve education in a difficult and challenging time for young people and not only.

Our base is located Athens because it is unique and dear to a large extent. Because of the opportunity to participate in its wise and beautiful development, that is why our actions bring us happiness.

Before starting many of our activities, we had to go through various preparations with the help of volunteers in organization, decoration, sales and promotion.

Alongside, we collaborated with various artists in painting, woodcarving, architecture, poetry, fairy tales, theater, etc.

That way, we function by continuously making preparations while running creative and artistic activities which evolve and open up doors to more possibilities.

Philosophy

Because an idea can be a clear vision to keep living the dream, we create a way where we connect traditions with modern knowledge and technology.

Within our everyday life we are able to express ourselves as much as possible with happiness and pleasure given by successful targets and healthy environments.

Our effort is to evolve with technology in the best way possible and achieve a way to work in a better environment.

Targets

As mentioned in the legislative statute:

The operation of a spiritual center, arts center, cafeteria and a center of various events with activities such as painting, sculpture, woodcarving, music teaching, concerts, chess lessons, etc.

The operation of a sales point for arts, souvenirs, post cards and other products where the income finances the organization’s activities.

Book publishing, newsletters, publications, running workshops, seminars and creating special printed publications.

Organizing trips, tours and accommodation in hotels and hostels in Greece and Bulgaria.

The contact and exchange of views, expertise, experiences and information with uniform or similar groups. Groups, Institutions, Associations and Unions, national and international. Implementation, management and observation of programs financed by the structural EU funds and programs of the Greek or Bulgarian government or other public or private entities.

The operation of an unemployment help center to find work.
