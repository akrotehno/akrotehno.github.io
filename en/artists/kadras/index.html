Giannis Kadras

Born in Tashkent in 1962.

He studied at the Superior School of Fine Arts (1979-1983) and the Higher School of Fine Arts (1985-2001). Since 1996 he is member of the Greek Chamber of Fine Arts. Lives and works in Athens.
