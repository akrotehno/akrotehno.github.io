Ikonomu Odisseas

Born in 1967 in Chrysodoli Ioannina.

From 1981 until 1985 he studied at the School of Fine Arts “Liceu Artistik”. He held free courses on art history and painting, taught by M. Stefanidis, I. Caruso, K. Markopoulo.

Member of the Chamber of Fine Arts. He has worked with famous galleries and his works are in private collections in Greece and abroad.

Collaborates with the “New Master Gallery”, USA. He has had solo exhibitions in Spyropouleio Cultural Centre of Law. Psychikou 1993 Tzimas gallery in 2005 and the Art Gallery Psychari 2006 and has participated in all the exhibitions organized by the Gallery Tzimas from 1997 until today, as and the Gallery Psychari 2006.
