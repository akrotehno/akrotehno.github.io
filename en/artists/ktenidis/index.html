Ktenidis Lazaros

Born in 1953 in Kazakhstan. He studied at the Fine Arts school of Kazakhstan and St. Petersburg in Russia. He taught as a professor of painting at the School of Fine Arts of Kazakhstan. He participated in group exhibitions held in Moscow, Alma-Ata and Tashkent. With his establishment in Greece 1990, he organized a painting workshop where he paints and teaches until today. He is a member of the artistic professional chamber Greece.
In Greece, he participated in group exhibitions such as: 1995 Group exhibition in Thessaloniki 1999 Cultural Center Melina Mercouri in Athens 2004 Aenaon Gallery in Athens 2006 Adam Art Gallery in Athens 2007 Group exhibition in Lamia 2009 Group exhibition in Athens
