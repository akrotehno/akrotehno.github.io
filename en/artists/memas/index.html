Stratos Memas

Born in 1968 in the town of Agirokastro. He finished higher school in literature and fine arts and graduated with excellent marks. During his student life he organized two personal exhibitions in the city of Elbasan and has participated in several group exhibitions in the city. From 1985 to 1988 he was honored with three first prizes at exhibitions of artistic association in Tepelene. In a new report in Agirokastro in 1985 he took the first prize while the national exhibition of young talents in Korca was awarded the second prize. He also participated in an international exhibition of young painters in Oslo, Norway. Since 1991 lives and works in Athens. His works are found in many galleries in Athens.

GROUP EXHIBITIONS
