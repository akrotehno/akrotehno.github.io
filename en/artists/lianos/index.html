Artistslianos
Born in 1975, Ipiro, Greece. Held free courses in painting and art history. In his student years he worked with caricature and also cooperated with the newspapers (having performed a solo exhibition of caricature). He has taken part in many group exhibitions. His works are in private collections in Greece and abroad. Member of the chamber of Fine Arts of Greece. Since 1992 lives and works in Athens.
