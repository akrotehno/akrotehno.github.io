Themistoklis Karakatsanis


My vision is a society where people do not compete with each other and with the assistance of our intellectual culture to make it possible for us all to have a better quality of life.


themistoklis.themis@gmail.com

History

Studies


Political and Economic Sciences. Graduate of the Panteion University of Athens. Sociology, Study of Philosophy, Financial Management, and Marketing.

Network Marketing


He deals with a modern business model called Network Marketing. The main reason he seriously deals with network marketing, is because he is convinced after serious and long-term study, that this way of promoting products is gradually eliminating competition in the market.
He has organized several seminars and lectures on the evolution of business and strongly believes that network marketing is the modern way of communication and work.
Seminars in ΕΒΕΑ on entrepreneurship and productivity.

Ganoderma


He has been dedicated to promote and provide information for therapeutic botanicals. One of these is Ganoderma Lucidum (grows in several mountains of Greece).

Music


Professional Musician - Singer. He collaborated with many great composers. One of them is Mikis Theodorakis.
He worked in a characteristic manner to disperse the good quality of Greek music in Europe and America.
Degree in music studies. Guitar, Piano, Vocal performance and Harmony

Voluntary work


Hosted free business seminars and lectures on the subject of personal improvement and development.
Concerts at Colleges and Universities abroad, and at Associations in Greece at no personal benefit, to spread good Greek music.

Other Interests


Knowledgeable about ancient Greek literature, fond of Poetry, Epicurean Philosophy, and contemporary Greek and foreign Literature. Literature works and poems have been published in several literature magazines.
He finds interest in organic farming and in his spare time engaged in the development of nature.
Systematic hiker, crossing many mountains in Greece and has gained much experience.
