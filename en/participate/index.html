Participate

The participation program is for all who are willing to practice the necessary skills on projects related to health, culture and arts. Along with our personal desire to organize ourselves and work as a team in the best possible way, bringing results in all the key areas that we wish to develop with our fair and worthy effort.

Please explore our website for a complete overview. Below you will find information on what we offer in our program.

We are open if you have any suggestions for cooperation or need advice. We will also be happy to hear your opinion or suggestion about our work.
