Akrotehno organizes creative activities since 2011. Before we get to where we are today, we present you significant projects which took place in other areas. Thank you all friends - participants who took part in our activities and wish you the best.

Sunday School Plaka Naxos Exarcheia

Sunday School

The Sunday school "Paisi Hilendarski" received recognition and support with the Akrotehno project by the Bulgarian Ministry of Education covering all costs of which are the salaries. The school is running for more than seven years and over 160 children get degrees.

Naxos

Three exhibitions in Filoti, Apeiranthos and the castle of "Agios Eleftherios." With the association of Filoti, a tourist trip was organized to the mountain of Zeus, Naxos.

Exarcheia

Our goal was to achieve within a critical time for the people, a place that is home to artists of all kinds and to present their creations. Within a few months, the place started to perform financially until we had to arrive in Lagonissi.

In 40 sq.m. there were many tasks needed to make the space functional. Designing, cleaning, painting, bar, toilets, attic, etc. Space insurance with value of 25000€ and outdoor decoration by Vangelis Hoursoglou, Woozy.
