Lagonissi

Right now in our space, we are organizing a cinematic film which also has elements of a documentary. At the same time, our space offers an ideal atmosphere to enjoy all the gifts of nature such as the flowers, fresh air, the sea, etc. Combined with a library, a room for music, and for computers with an open Internet connection.

Lagonissi is a seaside residential area in the southern part of Kalyvia Thorikou, Eastern Attica. Lagonissi is located approximately 30 kilometers southeast of Athens and 35 km northwest of Sounio.

With a 5 minute walk through a river path across us, you will get to a beautiful beach “Agios Nikolaos”. There you will find outdoor basketball, tennis and volleyball fields.

If you want to organize something special in relation to your artistic interests, or some other specific plan you have, we are open.

Directions

Athens-Sounio Avenue (Posidonos - National Road 91) connects Lagonissi with Athens and Sounio. Opposite the entrance to the beach “Agios Nikolaos”, is “Angelopoulos” street, turn there and then right onto “Papaflessa”. The first street on your left is “Filias”. We are at the corner.

Public Transportation

Coming from Athens, the bus “122” begins from the metro station “Elliniko” towards “Saronida”. Our nearest bus station is “Agios Nicholas”.
