Our Targets are:

Viability
Searching a unique, fair and pleasant way of working to bring happiness and pleasure by successful targets that are the concentration and stability in our evolution.
Creation
Finding ways in which our creative expression is the link between our favorite acts, tradition and modern knowledge.
Evolution
Improving human communication and lifestyle in healthy and productive areas with the support of modern technology and development of our natural environment.

Our Purpose :

Spaces
Spiritual center, arts center, cafeteria and a center of various events with activities such as painting, sculpture, woodcarving, music teaching, concerts, chess lessons, etc.
Information
Publishing books, newsletters, articles. Operating workshops and seminars. Creating special printed publications such as cd and dvd production for theater, cinema, etc..
Points of Sale
Operate a sales point for arts, souvenirs, post cards and other products where the income finances the organization activities.
Tourism
Organizing trips, tours and accommodation in hotels and hostels in Greece and Bulgaria.
Networking
The contact and exchange of views, expertise, experiences and information with uniform or similar groups. Groups, Institutions, Associations and Unions, national and international.
Job Vacancies
The operation of an unemployment help center to find work.
