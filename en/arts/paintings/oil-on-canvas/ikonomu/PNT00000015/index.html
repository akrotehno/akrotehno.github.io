Biography Summary

Ikonomu Odisseas

Born in 1967 in Chrysodoli Ioannina. From 1981 until 1985 he studied at the School of Fine Arts "Liceu Artistik". He held free courses on art history and painting, taught by M. Stefanidis, I. Caruso, K. Markopoulo. Member of the Chamber of Fine Arts. He has worked with famous galleries and his works are in private collections in Greece and abroad.
